Frequently Asked Questions — Painting in Coral Springs FL

How do I get HOA color approval for exterior painting in Coral Springs?

Most Coral Springs gated communities and planned neighborhoods require homeowners to submit a color change request to their HOA architectural review committee before repainting the exterior. We're familiar with this process throughout Coral Springs's neighborhoods and can provide paint chip samples and product data sheets formatted for HOA submission. We can also help you identify colors that are already on the approved palette so approval is a formality rather than a waiting game.

What does interior painting cost in a typical Coral Springs home?

For a standard 2,000–2,500 square foot Coral Springs home, interior painting of the main living areas, hallways, and bedrooms typically ranges from $3,500 to $6,500 depending on ceiling height, the number of accent walls, trim work complexity, and the number of doors. We provide free, room-by-room estimates so there are no surprises on pricing day.

Can you paint the exterior of my Coral Springs home while I'm still living there?

Yes — we work around occupied homes every day in Coral Springs. Our crew starts early, works systematically around the home's perimeter, and keeps walkways and entry doors clear and accessible at all times. Do you offer drywall repair before painting in Coral Springs FL?

Drywall repair and painting go hand in hand, and we handle both in a single mobilization.

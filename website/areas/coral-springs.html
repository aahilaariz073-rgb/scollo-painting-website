Coral Springs is one of Broward County's most established planned communities, with property types from the golf-course homes of Eagle Trace and Country Club to the neighborhoods of Wyndham Lakes and The Crossings. Coral Springs homes from the 1980s and 1990s present specific challenges: stucco with decades of accumulated paint layers, hardened caulking around windows, and drywall with hairline cracking from seasonal movement. Our prep addresses all of this — we grind and fill cracks, replace failed caulking, and apply elastomeric coatings where needed. The result is a finish that holds up rather than one that shows problems within a year.

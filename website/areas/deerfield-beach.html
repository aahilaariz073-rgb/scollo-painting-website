Q: What causes paint to blister and peel on Deerfield Beach stucco homes?

A: The most common cause of premature exterior paint failure on Deerfield Beach stucco homes is moisture intrusion — either through hairline cracks in the stucco that weren't properly repaired before painting, or through paint applied over a damp substrate. We always allow freshly washed stucco to fully dry before applying coatings, and we repair and prime all cracks with flexible elastomeric products before topcoating.

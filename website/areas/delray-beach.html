Delray Beach has one of the most diverse housing stocks in Palm Beach County — from the historic bungalows and Craftsman homes near Atlantic Avenue to newer gated communities west of I-95 and high-rise condos along the beachfront. Scollo Painting has decades of experience across all of these property types. We're comfortable with the detail-oriented work historic homes demand, the HOA color standards of planned communities, and the tight scheduling requirements of occupied condominiums.

Delray Beach's Atlantic coastal location means exterior projects need to be approached with salt air and UV exposure in mind. We power-wash every exterior surface to remove salt deposits, biological growth, and chalking paint before applying anything new. On older homes, we find failed caulking around windows that has been painted over multiple times — we remove and reapply all caulking rather than cover it, because old caulking will crack through the new paint within a season.

Exterior house painting in Delray Beach typically costs $3,500–$9,500 depending on size, stories, and surface condition. Interior projects vary significantly — a single room is typically $500–$900 while a full interior often runs $6,000–$14,000. Can you paint historic homes in Delray Beach without damaging original details?

Absolutely. Many of Delray Beach's older neighborhoods — including the historic districts downtown and near the beach — have original wood trim, built-in millwork, and architectural details that require careful masking and hand-painting. We're experienced with this type of work and take the time to properly coat architectural features rather than rushing through them.

Frequently Asked Questions — Painting in North Palm Beach FL

How does living on a canal or the Intracoastal affect paint longevity in North Palm Beach?

Waterfront homes in North Palm Beach — whether on a canal or directly on the Intracoastal — experience significantly accelerated paint film degradation compared to homes a mile or two inland. Salt air and wind-driven moisture are the primary culprits. We address this by using premium 100% acrylic or elastomeric exterior coatings with corrosion-inhibiting primers on any ferrous metal surfaces, and we recommend a recoating inspection every 5 years for Intracoastal-facing elevations regardless of how good the rest of the house looks.

What's the typical cost of exterior painting for a ranch-style North Palm Beach home?

A classic North Palm Beach ranch-style home of 1,500–2,200 square feet typically runs $3,000 to $5,500 for a complete exterior repaint including pressure washing, surface prep, and two finish coats of premium paint. Homes with extensive pool decks, screened enclosures, or ornate trim work may run higher. We provide free written estimates for every project in North Palm Beach.

How does the HOA color approval process work for exterior painting in Parkland?

In Parkland's gated communities — including Heron Bay, Parkland Golf & CC, and Miralago — homeowners must submit a written request to their HOA's architectural review committee before changing exterior paint colors. The request typically requires paint chip samples, the manufacturer's product name and number, and a description of which surfaces will be painted. We assist our Parkland customers by preparing a complete, professionally formatted color change package to maximize the chance of swift approval.

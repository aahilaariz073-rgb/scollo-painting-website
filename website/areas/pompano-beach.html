What's the best exterior paint for Pompano Beach's coastal climate?

For homes within a mile or two of the ocean, we recommend 100% acrylic elastomeric coatings or high-build acrylic latex with mildewcide additives. These products expand and contract with substrate temperature swings, resist algae and mold growth in Florida's humidity, and hold color far better under intense UV exposure than budget-grade paints.
